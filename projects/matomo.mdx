Matomo (formerly Piwik) is a full-featured, open source analytics platform that
provides you with 100% data ownership. Unlike hosted analytics solutions,
Matomo allows you to keep complete control of your data with powerful privacy
features while still offering the rich analytics insights you need.

## Key Features

### Complete Ownership

- Own your analytics data, no third-party access
- Host on your own servers or use Matomo Cloud
- No data limits or sampling
- Raw data access for advanced analysis
- Full data portability

### Privacy Focused

- GDPR and CCPA compliant analytics
- Cookie-less tracking option
- Data anonymization features
- Respect Do Not Track preferences
- Consent management
- Ability to track without consent under certain configurations

### Comprehensive Analytics

- Real-time visitor tracking
- User flow visualization
- Goal conversion tracking
- Ecommerce analytics
- Form analytics
- Content tracking
- Campaign tracking
- Custom variables and dimensions
- Custom reports

### Integration & Extensibility

- Hundreds of plugins available
- API for custom integrations
- Tag Manager for marketing tags
- Import data from Google Analytics
- Media analytics (video & audio)
- Heatmaps and session recordings
- A/B testing capabilities
- Mobile app analytics

Matomo gives organizations the insights they need to improve their websites and
marketing effectiveness without compromising on data ownership, privacy, or
compliance with regulations. It's trusted by over a million websites in more
than 190 countries.
