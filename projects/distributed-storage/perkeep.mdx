Perkeep (formerly Camlistore) is a personal storage system designed for long-term preservation of your data. It focuses on giving you control over your digital life while ensuring your memories and important files remain accessible for decades to come.

## Key Features

- **Content-Addressed Storage**:

  - Immutable blob storage
  - Content-based addressing
  - Automatic deduplication
  - Cryptographic integrity
  - Version tracking
  - Schema-free modeling

- **Personal Data Focus**:

  - Photo and media management
  - Document archival
  - Email preservation
  - Social media backup
  - Personal timeline
  - Memory preservation

- **Long-term Preservation**:

  - Format independence
  - Future-proof design
  - Migration tools
  - Data integrity verification
  - Multiple storage backends
  - Disaster recovery

- **Privacy & Control**:
  - Self-hosted solution
  - Complete data ownership
  - Encryption support
  - Access control
  - No vendor lock-in
  - Open standards
