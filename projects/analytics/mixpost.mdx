Mixpost is a self-hosted social media management platform focused on providing comprehensive
content scheduling and analytics capabilities without subscription fees or usage limits.
It enables teams to efficiently manage their social media presence through a centralized dashboard.

## Key Features

- **Content Management**:

  - Multi-platform scheduling
  - Post versioning
  - Media library
  - Content templates
  - Queue management
  - Calendar view
  - Hashtag groups

- **Team Collaboration**:

  - Dedicated workspaces
  - Task management
  - Permission controls
  - Team discussions
  - Performance monitoring
  - Workflow optimization

- **Analytics & Insights**:

  - Platform-specific metrics
  - Audience behavior tracking
  - Engagement analytics
  - Performance reporting
  - Custom dashboards
  - Data visualization

- **Advanced Capabilities**:
  - Dynamic variables
  - Automated posting
  - API integrations
  - Custom templates
  - Media optimization
  - Bulk scheduling

## Who Should Use Mixpost

Mixpost is ideal for:

- **Marketing Teams** needing centralized social management
- **Agencies** managing multiple client accounts
- **Businesses** wanting cost-effective social tools
- **Content Creators** requiring scheduling capabilities
