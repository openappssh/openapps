Digibunch is an educational-focused tool designed for creating and sharing curated collections of links with learners, colleagues, and collaborators. Built with Node.js and PHP, it provides educators and professionals with a simple yet powerful platform for organizing and distributing valuable web resources in an educational context.

## Key Features

- **Educational Link Curation**:

  - Create organized bunches of educational links
  - Curated collections for specific topics or courses
  - Educational resource organization and management
  - Topic-based grouping and categorization
  - Comprehensive resource compilation capabilities
  - Learning-focused link organization

- **Sharing & Collaboration**:

  - Easy sharing of link collections with others
  - Public collections for open educational resources
  - Collaborative curation with colleagues and students
  - Educational distribution and access management
  - Learner-friendly access to curated resources
  - Team-based resource sharing

- **Educational Workflow Integration**:

  - Learning resource management for educators
  - Course material organization and distribution
  - Student resource sharing and collaboration
  - Teacher tools for curriculum support
  - Educational workflow optimization
  - Academic resource curation

- **Intuitive Organization**:

  - Topic-based organization and structure
  - Category management for different subjects
  - Collection structure for logical grouping
  - Resource grouping by theme or purpose
  - Hierarchical organization capabilities
  - Flexible arrangement and sorting

- **User-Friendly Experience**:

  - Simple, intuitive interface design
  - Easy collection creation and management
  - Quick access to shared resources
  - Mobile-friendly responsive design
  - Intuitive navigation and discovery
  - Streamlined user experience

- **Management & Administration**:

  - Collection management and maintenance
  - Link validation and quality control
  - Content curation and organization
  - Access control and permissions
  - Usage analytics and insights
  - Administrative oversight capabilities

- **Educational Benefits**:
  - Enhanced learning resource discovery
  - Improved educational content organization
  - Collaborative learning support
  - Teacher productivity enhancement
  - Student engagement improvement
  - Knowledge sharing facilitation
