Akaunting is a comprehensive online accounting software designed for small businesses, freelancers, and entrepreneurs. It provides essential financial management tools including invoicing, expense tracking, financial reporting, and multi-company management in an intuitive web-based interface.

## Key Features

### Financial Management

- Double-entry accounting system
- Chart of accounts management
- Bank account reconciliation
- Cash flow tracking and management
- Financial statement generation
- Tax calculation and reporting

### Invoicing & Billing

- Professional invoice creation
- Recurring invoice automation
- Online payment integration
- Payment tracking and reminders
- Invoice templates and customization
- Multi-currency support

### Expense Management

- Expense categorization and tracking
- Receipt and document attachment
- Vendor and supplier management
- Bill payment scheduling
- Expense approval workflows
- Tax-deductible expense tracking

### Customer & Vendor Relations

- Customer database management
- Vendor information tracking
- Contact communication history
- Customer credit management
- Vendor payment terms
- Relationship analytics

### Reporting & Analytics

- Profit and loss statements
- Balance sheet generation
- Cash flow reports
- Tax summary reports
- Custom report creation
- Financial dashboard analytics

### Multi-Company Support

- Multiple business management
- Company switching interface
- Separate financial records
- Cross-company reporting
- User role management
- Data isolation

### Inventory Management

- Product and service catalog
- Stock level tracking
- Inventory valuation
- Purchase order management
- Supplier relationship tracking
- Low stock alerts

### Banking Integration

- Bank account connectivity
- Transaction import and sync
- Automatic categorization
- Bank reconciliation tools
- Payment processing integration
- Financial institution support

### Customization & Extensions

- App marketplace integration
- Custom field creation
- Workflow automation
- Theme and branding options
- API for integrations
- Plugin development framework

Akaunting simplifies business accounting by providing professional-grade financial management tools in an accessible format, making it ideal for businesses that need comprehensive accounting without complexity.
