Django-CRM is an open-source customer relationship management solution built on the Django framework. It aims to provide enterprise-level CRM capabilities while maintaining simplicity in development and customization by leveraging Django's built-in admin interface.

## Key Features

- **CRM Core Features**:

  - Lead and contact management
  - Deal tracking and sales forecasting
  - Company and product management
  - Payment tracking
  - Role-based access control

- **Task Management**:

  - Team tasks and projects
  - Task assignment and tracking
  - File sharing and chat integration
  - Office memos and reminders
  - Subtask management

- **Email Integration**:

  - Email marketing capabilities
  - Automated email synchronization
  - Newsletter management
  - Email templates and signatures
  - Mass mailing features

- **Analytics & Reporting**:
  - Sales analytics and forecasting
  - Income summary reports
  - Sales funnel analysis
  - Lead source tracking
  - Customizable reporting
