Odoo is a comprehensive suite of open source business applications that can be
used individually or integrated together to form a complete enterprise resource
planning (ERP) system. While it includes many business apps, its CRM
capabilities are robust and well-integrated with other business functions.

## Key Features

- **Business Applications**:

  - Full-featured CRM system
  - Website and eCommerce platform
  - Inventory and warehouse management
  - Project and task management
  - Accounting and invoicing
  - Point of sale system
  - Human resources management
  - Manufacturing management

- **CRM Capabilities**:

  - Lead and opportunity tracking
  - Sales pipeline management
  - Email marketing integration
  - Customer service management
  - Sales team automation
  - Performance analytics
  - Mobile CRM access

- **Integration Features**:

  - Cross-application workflows
  - Unified data model
  - Seamless navigation
  - Shared contact database
  - Integrated reporting
  - Single sign-on

- **Platform Features**:
  - Multi-company support
  - Role-based access control
  - REST API access
  - Extensive customization
  - Mobile applications
  - Localization support
