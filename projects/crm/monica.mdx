Monica is an open-source personal relationship management (PRM) system designed to help individuals document their lives and maintain meaningful relationships with family and friends. Unlike traditional CRMs, Monica focuses on personal rather than business relationships.

## Key Features

- **Contact Management**:

  - Comprehensive contact profiles
  - Relationship mapping
  - Custom contact fields
  - Activity logging
  - Contact labeling
  - Pet tracking

- **Personal Organization**:

  - Digital diary system
  - Document and photo storage
  - Task management
  - Multiple currency support
  - Multiple vaults
  - Custom activity types

- **Relationship Tools**:

  - Birthday reminders
  - Meeting history
  - Interaction tracking
  - Important date tracking
  - Contact favorites
  - Custom gender options

- **Privacy & Security**:
  - Self-hosted option
  - Data ownership
  - No social features
  - No tracking
  - Private by design
  - Multi-user support
