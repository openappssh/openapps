Ever Gauzy is an open-source business management platform designed for collaborative, on-demand and sharing economies. It combines enterprise resource planning (ERP), customer relationship management (CRM), human resource management (HRM) and project management capabilities in a single integrated solution.

## Key Features

- **HR Management**:

  - Employee profiles and management
  - Time tracking and attendance
  - Performance monitoring
  - Applicant tracking system
  - Employee onboarding tools
  - Time off management

- **Project Management**:

  - Task and project tracking
  - Team collaboration tools
  - Goals and KPI tracking
  - Activity monitoring
  - Schedules and appointments
  - Resource allocation

- **Business Operations**:

  - Customer relationship management
  - Sales pipeline management
  - Invoicing and billing
  - Expense tracking
  - Financial reporting
  - Inventory management

- **Platform Capabilities**:
  - Multi-organization support
  - Role-based permissions
  - Multi-currency support
  - Multi-language interface
  - Customizable dashboards
  - API integration options
