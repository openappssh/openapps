ERPNext is a comprehensive open source ERP system that includes robust CRM
capabilities along with a complete suite of business applications. It provides
an integrated platform for managing all aspects of business operations from
sales and customer relationships to accounting, HR and manufacturing.

## Key Features

- **Core Business Applications**:

  - Full accounting system
  - Order and inventory management
  - HR and payroll processing
  - Manufacturing operations
  - CRM and sales pipeline
  - Project management
  - Help desk
  - Asset management
  - Website builder

- **CRM Capabilities**:

  - Lead and opportunity tracking
  - Sales pipeline management
  - Customer communication history
  - Support ticket system
  - Email integration
  - Sales analytics
  - Customer portal

- **Platform Features**:

  - Multi-company support
  - Role-based permissions
  - Custom workflows
  - Report builder
  - REST API access
  - Mobile applications
  - Document management

- **Integration Capabilities**:
  - Payment gateways
  - Email servers
  - SMS gateways
  - REST APIs
  - Webhooks
  - Third-party apps
