Dolibarr is a comprehensive open source ERP and CRM solution that helps
organizations manage their business operations. It provides a modular approach
where users can enable only the features they need, making it suitable for
businesses of all sizes.

## Key Features

- **Business Management**:

  - Customer and supplier management
  - Product and service catalog
  - Stock and warehouse management
  - Sales pipeline and opportunities
  - Purchase orders and supplier management
  - Project and task tracking
  - HR and employee management
  - Accounting and financial management

- **CRM Capabilities**:

  - Contact and lead management
  - Commercial proposal handling
  - Contract and subscription tracking
  - Customer support ticketing
  - Email campaign integration
  - Calendar and scheduling
  - Document management
  - Partnership tracking

- **Technical Features**:

  - Multi-user with permissions
  - REST and SOAP APIs
  - Document generation (PDF/ODT)
  - Barcode integration
  - Email integration
  - Payment gateway support
  - LDAP connectivity
  - Mobile access

- **Additional Features**:
  - Multi-currency support
  - Multi-language interface
  - Customizable dashboards
  - Electronic document management
  - Mass emailing capabilities
  - Third-party integrations
  - Extensive reporting tools
