SuiteCRM is a comprehensive open-source CRM platform that provides enterprise-grade features for managing customer relationships, sales processes, marketing campaigns, and support operations. As a fork of SugarCRM's last open-source release, it has evolved into a robust solution with a strong community.

## Key Features

- **Sales Management**:

  - Lead and opportunity tracking
  - Quote and proposal generation
  - Sales pipeline management
  - Revenue forecasting
  - Product catalogs
  - Contract management

- **Marketing Tools**:

  - Campaign management
  - Email marketing
  - Target list creation
  - Campaign ROI tracking
  - Newsletter management
  - Marketing analytics

- **Customer Support**:

  - Case management
  - Knowledge base
  - Bug tracking
  - Customer portal
  - SLA management
  - Support workflows

- **Customization Options**:
  - Module builder
  - Field customization
  - Layout editor
  - Workflow automation
  - Custom reporting
  - Role-based access control
