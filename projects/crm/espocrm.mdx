EspoCRM is a comprehensive open-source CRM platform that helps organizations manage customer relationships effectively. It provides a clean, intuitive interface and extensive customization capabilities while maintaining robust functionality for sales, marketing, and support operations.

## Key Features

- **Customer Management**:

  - Contact and lead tracking
  - Account management
  - Activity logging
  - Document management
  - Calendar and scheduling
  - Task management

- **Sales Tools**:

  - Opportunity tracking
  - Sales pipeline management
  - Quote generation
  - Product catalogs
  - Revenue forecasting
  - Sales team management

- **Marketing Features**:

  - Email campaigns
  - Target list management
  - Mass email capabilities
  - Campaign tracking
  - Marketing analytics
  - Template management

- **Customization Options**:
  - Custom entity creation
  - Field customization
  - Layout management
  - Workflow automation
  - Formula fields
  - Dynamic forms
