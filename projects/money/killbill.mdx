Kill Bill is a comprehensive open-source subscription billing and payment platform designed for businesses requiring sophisticated billing capabilities. It provides extensive customization options and supports complex billing scenarios while maintaining full control over business and customer data.

## Key Features

- **Subscription Management**:

  - Flexible subscription plans
  - Usage-based billing
  - Trial management
  - Promotional pricing
  - Custom billing cycles
  - Plan changes and upgrades

- **Payment Processing**:

  - Multiple payment methods
  - Payment gateway integration
  - Dunning management
  - Refund handling
  - Chargeback processing
  - Payment routing

- **Technical Capabilities**:

  - REST API access
  - Plugin architecture
  - Multi-tenancy support
  - Analytics engine
  - Custom business rules
  - Extensive integrations

- **Business Tools**:
  - Financial reporting
  - Revenue analytics
  - Customer management
  - Tax handling
  - Multi-currency support
  - Audit trails
