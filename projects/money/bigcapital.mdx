BigCapital is an open-source accounting and inventory management system designed for small and medium businesses. It provides comprehensive financial tracking, reporting, and inventory control while being self-hostable and customizable to specific business needs.

## Key Features

- **Accounting Capabilities**:

  - Double-entry accounting system
  - Detailed financial reporting
  - Bank account reconciliation
  - Multi-currency transactions
  - Customizable chart of accounts

- **Inventory Management**:

  - Real-time inventory tracking
  - Purchase order management
  - Sales order processing
  - Stock level monitoring
  - Warehouse operations

- **Business Operations**:

  - Invoice generation and tracking
  - Bill payment management
  - Expense monitoring
  - Payment processing integration
  - Customer relationship management

- **Technical Features**:
  - Self-hosted option
  - Docker support
  - API integration
  - Multi-user support
  - Role-based access control
