InvoicePlane is a free, open-source invoicing and client management system designed for small businesses. It provides comprehensive tools for creating and managing invoices, tracking payments, and maintaining client relationships.

## Key Features

- **Invoice Management**:

  - Professional invoice creation
  - Quote and estimate generation
  - Custom invoice templates
  - Recurring invoices
  - Payment tracking
  - Tax handling

- **Client Management**:

  - Client database
  - Contact information
  - Payment history
  - Custom client fields
  - Client grouping
  - Email communication

- **Business Tools**:

  - Financial reporting
  - Payment tracking
  - Tax management
  - Multi-user access
  - Client portal
  - Custom fields

- **Technical Capabilities**:
  - Multi-language support
  - PDF generation
  - Email integration
  - Database support
  - Customizable templates
  - Simple deployment
