PrestaShop is a comprehensive e-commerce platform trusted by hundreds of thousands of merchants worldwide. It combines powerful features with flexibility, making it suitable for businesses of all sizes.

## Key Features

- **International Commerce**:

  - 75+ languages supported
  - Multi-currency handling
  - Tax management
  - Localized payment methods
  - International shipping
  - Regional settings

- **Advanced Management**:

  - Multi-store administration
  - Warehouse management
  - Advanced stock control
  - Supplier management
  - Employee permissions
  - Automated workflows

- **Rich Ecosystem**:

  - Official marketplace
  - Thousands of modules
  - Professional themes
  - Payment integrations
  - Service providers
  - Developer community

- **Marketing Power**:
  - Built-in SEO features
  - Email campaigns
  - Loyalty programs
  - Product recommendations
  - Social media integration
  - Analytics dashboard
