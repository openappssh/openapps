OpenCart is a feature-rich, easy-to-use open-source shopping cart solution. With its intuitive interface and extensive marketplace, it enables merchants to quickly set up and customize their online stores.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Store Capability**:

  - Manage multiple stores
  - Shared customer base
  - Centralized admin
  - Store-specific settings
  - Product sharing
  - Independent themes

- **User-Friendly Admin**:

  - Intuitive dashboard
  - Drag-and-drop interface
  - Bulk operations
  - Quick product entry
  - Order management
  - Built-in reports

- **Extensive Marketplace**:

  - Thousands of extensions
  - Payment integrations
  - Shipping modules
  - Professional themes
  - Marketing tools
  - One-click install

- **Built-in Features**:
  - Product comparisons
  - Customer reviews
  - Related products
  - Discount system
  - Tax management
  - Multi-language support
