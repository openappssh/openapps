CoreShop is a powerful e-commerce solution built as a plugin for Pimcore, combining the flexibility of Pimcore's data management platform with comprehensive e-commerce functionality. It's ideal for businesses needing tight integration between content and commerce.

## Key Features

- **Deep Pimcore Integration**:

  - Leverages Pimcore data objects
  - Integrated asset management
  - Document management system
  - Workflow automation
  - Multi-site capabilities
  - Unified backend

- **Comprehensive Commerce**:

  - Complete cart system
  - Order management
  - Payment gateway integration
  - Flexible shipping rules
  - Advanced tax handling
  - Invoice generation

- **Product Flexibility**:

  - Complex product variants
  - Dynamic attributes
  - Category hierarchies
  - Product relations
  - Bundle products
  - Digital products

- **Business Rules Engine**:
  - Price rule management
  - Discount campaigns
  - Voucher system
  - Customer group pricing
  - Quantity discounts
  - Time-based promotions
