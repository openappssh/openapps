vod2pod-rss is an innovative tool that converts YouTube and Twitch channels into podcast RSS feeds without requiring storage. It transcodes video content to MP3 audio on-the-fly and generates RSS feeds that can be consumed by any podcast client, enabling audio-only consumption of video content.

## Key Features

- **Channel Conversion**: Convert YouTube and Twitch channels into podcast RSS feeds
- **On-the-Fly Transcoding**: Real-time video-to-audio conversion without storage requirements
- **MP3 Output**: High-quality MP3 transcoding at 192k bitrate
- **RSS Generation**: Standard podcast RSS feeds compatible with all podcast clients
- **No Storage**: Streaming processing without local file storage
- **Docker Ready**: Simple Docker-based deployment and scaling

## Technical Specifications

- **Language**: Multiple (Docker-based)
- **License**: MIT
- **Deployment**: Docker containers
- **Architecture**: Streaming transcoding with RSS feed generation

## Use Cases

- **Video-to-Podcast**: Convert video channels to audio podcast format
- **Commuter Content**: Audio consumption of video content while traveling
- **Podcast Clients**: Use existing podcast apps for video channel content
- **Bandwidth Saving**: Audio-only consumption to save bandwidth
- **Background Listening**: Listen to video content without video

## Unique Advantages

- **No Storage**: On-the-fly processing without storage requirements
- **Universal Compatibility**: Works with any podcast client
- **Real-Time Processing**: Streaming transcoding for immediate access
- **Multi-Platform**: Supports both YouTube and Twitch
- **Docker-Based**: Easy deployment and scaling

⚠️ **Note**: This application depends on external video platforms and may be subject to their terms of service and availability.
