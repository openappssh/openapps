LidaTube is a specialized music collection enhancement tool designed to work seamlessly with Lidarr to identify and acquire missing albums from YouTube sources. Using yt-dlp as its backend, it automatically searches for and downloads missing content, helping users complete their music collections with content that may not be available through traditional sources.

## Key Features

- **Seamless Lidarr Integration**:

  - Direct integration with Lidarr installations
  - Automatic missing album detection and analysis
  - Real-time collection gap identification
  - Intelligent album prioritization and selection
  - Synchronized library updates and management
  - Compatible with existing Lidarr workflows

- **Intelligent YouTube Search and Matching**:

  - Advanced YouTube search algorithms
  - Album and artist matching logic
  - Track validation and verification
  - Quality assessment and selection
  - Duplicate detection and prevention
  - Search result ranking and optimization

- **Advanced Download Capabilities**:

  - yt-dlp backend for reliable downloading
  - High-quality audio extraction and conversion
  - Multiple format support and selection
  - Metadata preservation and enhancement
  - Automatic file organization and naming
  - Resume and retry functionality

- **Comprehensive Automation**:

  - Automatic missing album scanning
  - Scheduled search and download operations
  - Background processing and queue management
  - Progress tracking and status updates
  - Error handling and recovery mechanisms
  - Continuous monitoring and updates

- **Smart Collection Management**:

  - Gap analysis and completion tracking
  - Album prioritization based on preferences
  - Collection statistics and reporting
  - Download history and success tracking
  - Quality management and optimization
  - Library synchronization and updates

- **Flexible Configuration**:

  - Customizable search preferences and criteria
  - Quality settings and format selection
  - Download scheduling and automation options
  - Lidarr connection and authentication setup
  - Custom matching rules and filters
  - Performance tuning and optimization

- **Self-Hosting Benefits**:
  - Complete control over music collection completion
  - Privacy protection for music acquisition activities
  - Custom configuration for specific collection needs
  - Integration with existing music automation infrastructure
  - No dependency on external music services
  - Enhanced collection completeness and availability
