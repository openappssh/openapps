13Feet is an open source tool that helps bypass article paywalls by leveraging Google's cached versions of web pages. It provides a clean, distraction-free reading experience by removing ads, popups, and other intrusive elements.

## Key Features

### Paywall Bypass

- Access paywalled content through Google cache
- Clean article view without ads or popups
- Support for most major news sites
- Simple URL-based interface

### Privacy Focus

- No tracking or data collection
- No account required
- Anonymous access
- No cookies needed

### Simple Integration

- Easy to use web interface
- Browser bookmark support
- API access available
- Mobile-friendly design

### Self-Hosting Benefits

- Complete control over the service
- No rate limits or restrictions
- Privacy-preserving deployment
- Customizable configuration

13Feet provides a valuable tool for researchers, students, and readers who need access to paywalled content while maintaining their privacy. Its simple deployment makes it an excellent choice for organizations wanting to provide this service to their users.
