ITFlow is a comprehensive business management platform specifically designed for Managed Service Providers (MSPs). It combines ticketing, IT documentation, invoicing, and accounting capabilities into a single, integrated solution that helps MSPs streamline their operations and deliver better service to their clients.

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive Ticketing System**:

  - Full-featured ticket management
  - Client portal for ticket submission
  - SLA tracking and management
  - Time tracking and billing
  - Priority and status management
  - Automated workflows

- **IT Documentation Management**:

  - Centralized IT documentation
  - Asset and inventory management
  - Network documentation and diagrams
  - Password and credential management
  - Knowledge base creation
  - Document templates

- **Financial Management**:

  - Integrated invoicing system
  - Accounting and bookkeeping
  - Expense tracking and management
  - Payment processing integration
  - Financial reporting and analytics
  - Tax management

- **Client Relationship Management**:

  - Comprehensive client database
  - Contact and communication management
  - Service agreement tracking
  - Client portal access
  - Communication history
  - Client performance metrics

- **Business Analytics**:

  - Performance dashboards
  - Business intelligence reports
  - Financial analytics
  - Client profitability analysis
  - Service delivery metrics
  - Custom reporting tools

- **MSP-Specific Features**:

  - Service agreement management
  - Recurring billing automation
  - Multi-client management
  - Technician scheduling
  - Remote monitoring integration
  - Compliance tracking

- **Integration Capabilities**:
  - Email system integration
  - Accounting software connectivity
  - Payment gateway integration
  - Third-party tool connections
  - API access for custom integrations
  - Webhook support

## Technical Specifications

- **Language**: PHP
- **Database**: MySQL/MariaDB
- **Web Server**: Apache, nginx
- **Frontend**: Modern web interface
- **API**: RESTful API
- **License**: GPL-3.0
- **Platforms**: Linux, Windows (via web server)

## Use Cases

- **Managed Service Providers**: Complete business management for MSP operations
- **IT Consultants**: Client management and service delivery tracking
- **Small IT Businesses**: Integrated business operations platform
- **IT Departments**: Internal IT service management and documentation
- **Technical Support**: Comprehensive support ticket and client management
- **Business Operations**: Financial and operational management for IT services

## Unique Advantages

- **MSP-Focused**: Specifically designed for Managed Service Provider needs
- **All-in-One Solution**: Combines ticketing, documentation, and financial management
- **Cost Effective**: Free alternative to expensive MSP platforms
- **Self-Hosted**: Complete control over business data and operations
- **Integrated Workflow**: Seamless integration between all business functions
- **Open Source**: Customizable and extensible platform

Based on the [ITFlow GitHub repository](https://github.com/itflow-org/itflow), this tool provides MSPs with a comprehensive, cost-effective alternative to expensive commercial platforms, offering all the essential features needed to run a successful IT service business in a single, integrated solution.
