AdventureLog is a comprehensive travel tracking and trip planning application that puts your travel memories and future adventures in one organized place. Perfect for documenting journeys, planning trips, and maintaining a personal travel history.

## Key Features

- **Travel Planning**:

  - Trip itinerary creation
  - Destination research tools
  - Planning templates
  - Budget tracking
  - Activity scheduling
  - Route planning

- **Journey Tracking**:

  - Real-time location tracking
  - GPS trail recording
  - Travel timeline
  - Distance calculations
  - Speed monitoring
  - Waypoint marking

- **Memory Documentation**:

  - Photo integration
  - Travel notes and journals
  - Experience ratings
  - Memory tagging
  - Story compilation
  - Timeline visualization

- **Organization & Management**:
  - Trip categorization
  - Search and filtering
  - Export capabilities
  - Data backup
  - Privacy controls
  - Multi-user support
