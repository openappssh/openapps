AirTrail is a specialized personal flight tracking system designed for aviation enthusiasts, pilots, and frequent flyers who want to maintain detailed records of their flights and analyze their flying patterns.

## Key Features

- **Flight Management**:

  - Comprehensive flight logging
  - Route recording and tracking
  - Aircraft information database
  - Airport details and codes
  - Flight duration tracking
  - Departure and arrival times

- **Data Visualization**:

  - Interactive flight maps
  - Route visualization
  - Flight path tracking
  - Geographic coverage
  - Timeline views
  - Statistical charts

- **Analytics & Statistics**:

  - Total flight hours
  - Distance calculations
  - Most visited airports
  - Flight frequency analysis
  - Performance metrics
  - Travel patterns

- **Personal Records**:
  - Private flight history
  - Personal notes and memories
  - Photo attachments
  - Flight ratings
  - Experience documentation
  - Achievement tracking
