ZenTao is a comprehensive agile project management system that provides end-to-end support for the software development lifecycle. With its focus on Scrum methodology and integrated approach to product management, project execution, and quality assurance, it offers teams a complete solution for agile development.

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive Product Management**:

  - Product planning and strategy
  - User story management and tracking
  - Release planning and coordination
  - Product roadmap visualization
  - Feature prioritization and tracking
  - Product backlog management

- **Advanced Project Management**:

  - Detailed project planning tools
  - Sprint management and execution
  - Task assignment and tracking
  - Milestone planning and monitoring
  - Progress tracking and reporting
  - Resource allocation and management

- **Integrated Quality Assurance**:

  - Test case creation and management
  - Test execution and tracking
  - Bug tracking and resolution
  - Quality metrics and reporting
  - Testing workflow automation
  - QA process management

- **Scrum Methodology Support**:

  - Complete Scrum framework implementation
  - Sprint planning and execution
  - Burndown charts and metrics
  - Velocity tracking and analysis
  - Agile reporting and analytics
  - Scrum ceremony support

- **Team Collaboration Tools**:

  - Team organization and management
  - Document management system
  - Communication and discussion tools
  - Activity feeds and tracking
  - Notification and alert system
  - Knowledge sharing features

- **Reporting & Analytics**:

  - Comprehensive project reporting
  - Progress and performance analytics
  - Custom report generation
  - Dashboard and visualization
  - Metrics tracking and analysis
  - Data export capabilities

- **Enterprise Features**:
  - Multi-project management
  - User and permission management
  - Workflow customization
  - Integration capabilities
  - Backup and maintenance tools
  - Scalable architecture
