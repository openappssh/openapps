Titra is a comprehensive time-tracking and project management solution specifically designed for freelancers and small teams. With its focus on simplicity and effectiveness, it provides all the essential tools needed to track time, manage projects, and generate reports for better productivity and billing.

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive Time Tracking**:

  - Accurate time recording and tracking
  - Project-based time tracking
  - Task-level time management
  - Manual time entry options
  - Built-in timer functionality
  - Time tracking history and logs

- **Project Management Tools**:

  - Project organization and structure
  - Task creation and management
  - Client and customer management
  - Project planning and scheduling
  - Progress tracking and monitoring
  - Project timeline visualization

- **Advanced Reporting**:

  - Detailed time reports
  - Project performance reports
  - Client-specific reports
  - Export capabilities (PDF, CSV, Excel)
  - Analytics and insights
  - Custom report generation

- **Team Collaboration**:

  - Team member management
  - User roles and permissions
  - Collaborative project features
  - Team time tracking
  - Team performance reports
  - Communication tools

- **Freelancer-Focused Features**:

  - Client billing and invoicing
  - Automated invoice generation
  - Hourly rate management
  - Expense tracking and management
  - Freelancer dashboard and overview
  - Revenue tracking and analysis

- **User Experience**:

  - Intuitive, user-friendly interface
  - Mobile device support
  - Fast performance and response
  - Easy navigation and workflow
  - Responsive web design
  - Minimal learning curve

- **Business Features**:
  - Multi-currency support
  - Tax calculation and management
  - Budget tracking and alerts
  - Productivity analytics
  - Goal setting and tracking
  - Performance metrics
